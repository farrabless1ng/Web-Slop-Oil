About us

Slop & Oil Recovery, S.A. is a reputable Panamanian firm excelling in hydrocarbon waste management for 23 years. We specialize in spill prevention, transportation, tank cleaning, and more. Our services include bioremediation, training, contingency planning, and retailing absorbents. Committed to safety, We operate 24/7, meeting stringent regulations.

Services

Our services encompass everything from prevention to the cleanup of hydrocarbon spills, including the collection of oily waters, tank cleaning, handling of contaminated materials, and soil bioremediation. Additionally, we offer specialized training in hazardous materials operations and emergency response, as well as the development of contingency and management plans. With a comprehensive approach, we are committed to safeguarding the maritime and terrestrial environment, providing effective and sustainable solutions for businesses and industries.
