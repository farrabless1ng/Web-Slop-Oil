About us

Slop & Oil Recovery, S.A. is a Panamanian company with a rich history and an unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation. Founded in 1997, we have been at the forefront of the industry for over two decades."

Our commitment

We offer a wide range of specialized services designed to meet the unique needs of our clients. We take pride in operating 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, and strictly adhere to our clients' schedules nationwide

Compliance with Regulations

We take pride in complying with all current health and safety regulations in accordance with applicable national and international regulations in the sector. In the maritime aspect, we adhere to international regulations and current legislation through the Panama Maritime Authority (PMA). On the terrestrial side, our activities are oriented in accordance with Law No. 6 of January 11, 2007, regulated by the Ministry of Health (MINSA), in addition to complying with the General Environmental Law and other requirements demanded by the National Environmental Authority (ANAM).
